PER-IMAGE SONGS  (optional)
===========================
Each photo in "The riddle reel" carousel can play its own song.
Just drop mp3 files here with these exact names:

  c1.mp3  →  teal shirt, garden (sunny & unbothered)
  c2.mp3  →  neon green mirror selfie
  c3.mp3  →  Taj Mahal, black dress
  c4.mp3  →  Holi, colourful hands
  c5.mp3  →  mountains, cream shawl
  c6.mp3  →  hilltop selfie, blue hoodie
  c7.mp3  →  teal shirt close-up
  c8.mp3  →  Christmas tree
  c9.mp3  →  blue doorway, Rajasthan

Then click "♪ play its song" on that slide.
If a file is missing, the site just shows a gentle note — nothing breaks.

BACKGROUND MUSIC:  drop assets/song.mp3  (tap the ♪ button, bottom-right)
VIDEO:             drop assets/video.mp4 (shows in "A little clip")
